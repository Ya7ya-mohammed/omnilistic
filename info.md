# Omnilistic Cards Collection

A collection of custom Lovelace cards for Home Assistant.

## Included Cards

- **omnilistic-speedtest** - Display speedtest results
- **omnilistic-card** - Main omnilistic interface card
- **minimal-purifier-card** - Clean air purifier control card

## Installation

All cards will be available in `www/community/omnilistic-cards/` after installation.
